Fried Rice

Description

This recipe is a wonderful way to reuse leftover rice.

Alongside that, it's a great method of introducing vegetables into your diet!

Ingredients

NOTE: This recipe places focus on the rice. The meat can be seasoned your own way. This also applies to the rice.

  • Oil
  • Meat
  • Cold, cooked rice
  • vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Oyster sauce

Steps

  1. Add oil and heat the pan.
  2. Cook and season the meat until it's almost done.
  3. Remove the meat. Add more oil.
  4. Cook the vegetables for a few minutes.
  5. Add rice and cook for a few more minutes.
  6. Add soy sauce and oyster sauce.
  7. Add meat. Cook until meat is finished.
  8. Serve.
