Loaded Fries

Description

A super simple one. Anyone can do this. It really does help to spice up your fries.

Ingredients

  • Uncooked Fries
  • Cheese
  • OPTIONAL: bacon

Steps

  1. Cook the fries, adding whatever seasonings you want.
  2. Add the cheese to the top of the still-hot fries
  3. Add the bacon atop the melted cheese
  4. Serve.
